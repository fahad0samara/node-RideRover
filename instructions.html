Welcome to our Bike Catalog!

Below are the instructions on how to use our catalog:

  1. Explore our wide range of bikes by visiting the Bikes page.
  2. To view bikes by category, navigate to the Categories page.
  3. To view all bikes without pagination, visit the All Bikes page.
  4. To view details of a single bike:
    • Visit the bike's page by clicking on its name or image from the Bikes page.
    • The bike's page will display detailed information about the bike.
